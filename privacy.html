Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 1, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Build & Chill Autoposter ("the App") handles your information when you connect your TikTok account.

1. Information We Access

When you connect your TikTok account, the App may access:

  • Account info: username and profile avatar (to confirm account identity in the dashboard)
  • Video list: your published TikTok videos (to display analytics and engagement metrics)
  • Analytics data: views, likes, shares, and other performance metrics on your videos

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Display your account in the personal dashboard
  • Show video performance metrics for your own reporting
  • Publish video content on your behalf when you schedule a post

We do not sell, share, or transmit your data to any third parties.

3. Data Storage

All OAuth access tokens and account credentials are stored locally on your device. No personal data is sent to or stored on external servers controlled by this App.

4. TikTok API Compliance

This App uses the TikTok API in accordance with TikTok Developer Platform Terms and TikTok's Terms of Service. Data accessed through the TikTok API is used only for the purposes disclosed above.

5. Data Deletion

You can revoke the App's access to your TikTok account at any time via TikTok's account settings under Settings > Security > Manage App Permissions. Revoking access immediately terminates the App's ability to access your account.

6. Children's Privacy

This App is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page reflects any revisions. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

8. Contact

For privacy questions or data deletion requests, contact: dan@buildchill.com
